Delete Your WanderCrew Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your WanderCrew account, you can do so using any of the methods below.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent. All your data, including trip information, messages, and personal settings will be permanently removed from our systems.

How to Delete Your Account

1. From Within the App

The easiest way to delete your account is directly through the WanderCrew app:

  1. Open the WanderCrew app
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Select Account Settings tab
  4. Scroll down to the Danger Zone section
  5. Tap Delete Account
  6. Confirm your decision

2. Using Our Website

You can also delete your account through our website:

  1. Go to our account deletion page
  2. Sign in with your WanderCrew account
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to delete your account
Delete Your Account

3. Contact Our Support Team

If you're having trouble deleting your account through the app or website, please contact our support team:

  • Email: privacy@groupwanderverse.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Include your registered email address

We'll process your request within 7 business days.
